Susan

Backstory:

Susan is Karens sister and the aunt to the victims children. Karen has been said to be a person who would do anything for her family.

Suspicion:

Karen and the victim were known for getting into arguments and everyone in the family was aware that they didnt get along. This cruise was supposed to be a break from all the fighting but as soon as they got on the boat they were already beginning to argue with eachother about what to do. Karen got so mad that she ran away from the argument and her family went to look for her with no success. The detectives were able to find her later in the kitchen talking to the head chef Kevin.

Statement:
